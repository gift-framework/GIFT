# Code of Conduct

## Summary

Be respectful, collaborative, and maintain scientific integrity.

## Expected Behavior

- Professional and respectful discourse
- Constructive feedback on ideas
- Honest about limitations and uncertainties
- Proper citation of sources

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation
- Deliberate misinformation

## Enforcement

Report issues via GitHub. Violations may result in warnings or bans.

Inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
